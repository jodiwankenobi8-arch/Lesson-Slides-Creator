## ‚ùì COMMON QUESTIONS

**Q: Do I need to learn all these shortcuts?**

**Q: What if I forget a shortcut?**  
A: Press **?** during teaching to see the list.

**Q: Can I still use my mouse?**  
A: Absolutely! Shortcuts are optional but faster.

**Q: What if I accidentally delete a lesson?**  
A: Just click "Recently Deleted" and restore it. You have 30 days.

**Q: Does offline mode work for new lessons?**  
A: You need to create the lesson while online first. After that, it works offline.

**Q: Will this slow down my old laptop?**  
A: Opposite! The app now loads faster on old laptops.
